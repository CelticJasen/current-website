About us

Current, Inc. has been serving West Plains and the surrounding communities since 1989. We specialize in Electrical, Heating & Air Conditioning, residential and commercial service, repair and new installations. We also provide reliable, high speed internet, and IT service to our local community. Our company offers the quality and reliable workmanship you can count on. In addition, our friendly and professional staff is here to answer any questions you may have about any product or service we offer. Please call us @ 417-257-0504 and speak to a professional today who can schedule a service call or new installation or a totally free estimate.

Current, Inc. offers the highest quality of residential and commercial products and services on the market today. Since 1989, we have provided superior service to our customers and have assisted them in achieving their goals. Our many years of combined experience and commitment to excellence have earned us the reputation as the best Electrical, Heating & Air Contractor in the area.

Danny and Stefanie Thomas of Summersville, MO are the proud owners of Current Inc. Danny has worked for the company since 2007 after graduating from Linn State Technical College, and purchased the company in 2015. The couple has been married since 2007, and have two boys, Zachary and Trenton. They are all very active in their local church, and always put God first in everything they do.
